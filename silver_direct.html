The Direct-Line Descendants of Archibald Silver Sr.
Generation 1: Archibald Silver Senior (1630-1685):

Archibald was bron in 1630 in Fetteresso, Kincardineshire, Scotland. He died in 1703 in Manneton Creek, Salem County, New Jersey (Based on will). He married Christian Cheyne in Matacopine, West New Jersey. She was born in SCotland and arrived at East New Jersey, as an indentured servant to George Keith, Feb 1685. She died in New Jersey.

Generation 2: Archibald Silver II (1696-1769):

Archibald II was born in 1696 in New Jersey. He died on October 1769 in Salem County, New Jersey. He married Mary Cowgill, daughter of Ralph Cowgill and Susannah Pancoast, on April 14 1720 in Burlington, New Jersey. She was born on January 7 1700 in Burlington, Burlington County, New Jersey. She died on November 3 1767 in New Jersey.

Generation 3: Samuel Silver (1731-1808):

Samuel Silver was born on January 18 1731 in New Jersey. He died on 23 May 1808 in New Jersey. He married Rebeckah Pedrick, daughter of Thomas Pedrick and Rebecca Fawcett, on October 31 1765 in Salem, New Jersey. She was born on April 11 1745 in Salem, Salem County, New Jersey. She died on October 4 1823 in Pilesgrove, Salem County, New Jersey.

Generation 4: William Silver (1773-1848):

Wlliam Silver was born on March 18 1773 in Salem, Salem County, New Jersey. He died on 23 May 1848 in Camden, Preble County, Ohio. He married Rebecca Bates, daughter of William Bates Jr. and Phebe Butcher, on January 12 1797 in Lower Alloways Creek Friends Meeting House, Salem County, New Jersey. She was born on March 4 1776 in New Jersey. She died on December 12 1849 in Preble, Ohio.
