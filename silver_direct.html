The Direct-Line Descendants of Archibald Silver Sr.
Generation 1: Archibald Silver Senior (1630-1685):

Archibald was bron in 1630 in Fetteresso, Kincardineshire, Scotland. He died in 1703 in Manneton Creek, Salem County, New Jersey (Based on will). He married Christian Cheyne in Matacopine, West New Jersey. She was born in SCotland and arrived at East New Jersey, as an indentured servant to George Keith, Feb 1685. She died in New Jersey.

Generation 6: Robert A. Silver (1849-1930):

Robert A. Silver was born on January 25 1849 in Preble County, Ohio. He died on December 14 1930 in Knightstown, Henry County, Indiana and is buried at Crown Hill Cemetery, Indianapolis, Indiana. He married Laura S. Lawrence, daughter of Henry Lawrence and Elizabeth Coblentz, on October 9 1879 in Indianapolis, Marion County, Indiana. Their wedding announcement stated that Reverend Jones, United Brethren Church, officiated the wedding. The wedding was held at her parents' house, at 35 Plum Street, Indianapolis, Indiana. She was born on September 16 1856 in Eldorado, Darke County, Ohio. She died on January 20 1927 in Knightstown, Henry County, Indiana. She died at the family home at the corner of Morgan and Franklin Streets, and was buried in Crown Hill Cemetery, Indianapolis Indiana.

Generation 7: Ralph Lawrence Silver (1886-1959):

Ralph Lawrence Silver was born on January 7 1886 in Indianapolis, Marion County, Indiana. He died on January 27 1959 in Indianapolis. His residence at the time of his death was 3710 N. Meridian Street, and he was an Engineer for the State Highway Department. He married Dora "Dorothy" Arnold. She was born on October 4 1888 in Knightstown, Henry County, Indiana. She died on September 13 1963 in Indianapolis, Indiana. She had been a music teacher. They are both buried at Glen Cove Cemetery, Knightstown, Indiana.
